Olga Sudareva

Professional Teaching Fellow, Department of Economics, University of Auckland, New Zealand.

I am an economist whose research lies at the intersection of Macroeconomics and Labour Economics. I study how social norms, institutional environments, and technological change shape economic development and inequality. My recent work also explores how advances in artificial intelligence can enhance economic analysis and policy evaluation.
